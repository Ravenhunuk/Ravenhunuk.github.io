What is YUMEIHO THERAPY?

​Yumeiho Massage Therapy is like a Japanese physiotherapy. It is a pressure-kneading, full-body, deep muscle massage consisting of over hundred steps and joint correction movements. The purpose of the massage is to treat and correct the asymmetric and therefore disharmonic human body, thereby activating self-healing, to prevent diseases and to heal existing disorders. Its effects are essentially long-lasting and affects the entire body, helping to unblock the flow of energy in the meridians and triggering the body’s self-healing functions. The benefits of the therapy can be seen as early as the first treatment: back, waist and neck pain gradually grow faint or disappear, muscle tension eases around the vertebrae and joints, movements gradually refines, and its also detoxify the body. During the treatment you are fully dressed in comfortable clothes like joggers and T-shirt. The therapy is being performed on a special mattress placed on the floor.

Introduction

In 2017 I started to receive Yumeiho Therapy from a friend of mine. I tried lots of massage therapies before, but I did not find any techniques that were effective and complex as Yumeiho therapy. In order to be able to help other people I have decided to learn the appropriate skills and techniques this massage style. In 2018 I have received my first dan diploma in Hungary and In 2020 I was qualified as an official 3 dan therapist. Right after I opened my own practice. Since then I have gained more knowledge in the alternative medicine field such as cupping therapy, ear acupuncture and the base of Tradicional Chinese Medicine (TCM). Despite the fact that I’m mainly treating musculoskeletal problems at the moment, I’m looking forward to learn more about TCM and build it in my treatments. My aim during a session is to understand where the problems came from and treat them according to my best knowledge.
